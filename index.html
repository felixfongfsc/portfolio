About

I’m Felix Fong, a UI/UX designer and student in the BSc Creative Media programme at City University of Hong Kong. With two years of experience from self‑initiated and competition projects, I focus on creating simple, accessible, and minimalistic digital experiences. Most of my work is built in Figma, while I use Procreate and GoodNotes 5 for quick low‑fidelity sketching. I am currently looking for a chance to bring clean, user‑centred designs to real‑world products.
